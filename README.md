# Blick-watchOS
Control your shot. Capture the moment. Right from your wrist.  Blick for Wear OS is the ultimate companion for your mobile camera. Leave your phone on a tripod or lean it against a wall—your watch becomes your viewfinder and your remote trigger.

Turn your phone into a professional remote-controlled lens.
Blick transforms your Android device into a high-performance camera hub designed to be controlled entirely from your wrist. Using a low-latency wireless bridge, it streams a live, high-quality viewfinder directly to your Wear OS watch.
